If you purchased the laptop and it still has the old user account on it, follow these steps to reset it and set it up as new:

---

### 1. Get to the Sign-In Screen

- Turn on the laptop.
    
- If you see a login screen asking for a **PIN or password**, stop there (you don’t need to log in).
    

---

### 2. Open the Recovery Options

1. On the login screen, **hold down the Shift key** on the keyboard.
    
2. While holding Shift, click the **Power icon** (bottom right corner of the screen).
    
3. Select **Restart**.
    
4. Keep holding Shift until you see a blue screen that says **Choose an option**.
    

---

### 3. Start the Reset

1. On the blue screen, click **Troubleshoot**.
    
2. Select **Reset this PC**.
    
3. Choose **Remove everything**.
    
    - This will erase all files, accounts, and settings, giving you a fresh start.
        
4. Select **Local reinstall** (usually quicker).
    

---

### 4. Complete the Setup

- The laptop will restart several times—this can take 20–60 minutes.
    
- When it’s done, you’ll see the **Windows setup screen**.
    
- Follow the steps to create your own account and password.
    

---

✅ After this, the laptop will be clean and ready for use.
