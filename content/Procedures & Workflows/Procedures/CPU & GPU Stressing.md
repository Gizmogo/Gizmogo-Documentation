#Testing 

---

| Goal                         | Duration   | Notes                                           |
| ---------------------------- | ---------- | ----------------------------------------------- |
| Basic performance validation | 30 minutes | Quick check for temps and throttling            |
| Moderate stress test         | 1–2 hours  | Good balance for most laptops                   |
| Full burn-in / validation    | 4–8 hours  | Rarely necessary unless diagnosing major issues |
### 🔥 Key Things to Monitor During the Test

- **CPU Temperature**: Keep it below 90°C (ideally <85°C) — laptops can get hot fast.
    
- **Throttling**: Check if the CPU is downclocking under load (AIDA64 shows this).
    
- **Battery behavior**: Make sure the laptop is on **AC power**, not battery, during the test.
    
- **Fan noise and ramp-up**: Make sure fans respond appropriately under load.

### ⚠️ Tips & Warnings

- **Don’t run the GPU and CPU stress tests simultaneously** on most laptops — this often causes unnecessary thermal throttling or shutdown.
    
- **Watch for shutdowns or BSODs** — they indicate hardware instability, faulty cooling, or bad RAM.
