>[!Attention]
>Use the following notes if an item was evaluated and found to be an imitation of popular or high end Brand

```
After thorough examination and analysis, it has been determined that the device in question exhibits characteristics inconsistent with genuine products manufactured by [manufacturer's name]. Upon close inspection, discrepancies were identified in the device's build quality, labeling, packaging, and/or functionality, indicating that it is likely a counterfeit imitation.
```

## Apple

```
After thorough examination and analysis, it has been determined that the device in question exhibits characteristics inconsistent with genuine products manufactured by Apple. Upon close inspection, discrepancies were identified in the device's build quality, labeling, packaging, and/or functionality, indicating that it is likely a counterfeit imitation.
```
