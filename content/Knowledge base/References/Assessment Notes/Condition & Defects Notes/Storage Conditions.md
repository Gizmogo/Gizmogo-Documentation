## Many Sector Errors
```
the hard drive contains reallocated sectors, indicating a high risk of imminent drive failure.
```
## Few Incorrect sectors
```
the storage drive contains reallocated and incorrect sectors, suggesting early signs of drive degradation.
```
## Slow Drive
```
the storage drive is performing below average, indicating a potential hardware fault and an increased risk of failure.
```
