Below are a list of notes to be used for the various condition and defect found on different types of inputs and outputs.

## non-functional
```
the device’s ports are defective and cannot be used for charging or data transfer.
```
## unstable connection
```
the unit fails to maintain a stable connection and can disconnect when slightly shifted, indicating a loose or failing internal connection.
```
