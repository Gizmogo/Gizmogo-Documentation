## Defective integrated camera

```
The device has passed general testing procedures and successfully boots without issue, confirming that its core system functions are operational. However, the built-in camera is nonfunctional and fails to operate as intended. Since the camera is an integral component of the device’s overall functionality, its failure significantly reduces usability.
```

## Shutter issue.

```
The camera encounters an error after capturing an image and prompts the user to power the device off and on. However, the unit does not respond to the power command and must have the battery removed to force a shutdown. This behavior suggests a potential issue with the shutter mechanism or internal hardware and significantly impacts overall usability.
```
