# iPods
## Brand New:
-	Factory sealed in original Apple box
-	Never activated or used
-	Includes all original accessories (cable, earbuds, manuals)
-	No cosmetic or functional issues
## Like New:
-	Looks flawless — no scratches, dents, or scuffs
-	Screen crystal clear — no cracks or dead pixels
-	Buttons, ports, speakers, and touchscreen all work perfectly
-	Fully functional
## Good:
-	Light cosmetic wear (small scuffs or faint marks)
-	Screen in great shape — no cracks or major blemishes
-	All functions (music, Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, camera) work properly
-	Fully functional
## Fair:
-	Noticeable wear (scratches, dents, or faded paint)
-	Screen lights up with no major display issues
-	May have minor cosmetic flaws but works normally
-	All key features (touch, sound, charging, etc.) functional
-	Fully functional
## Damaged:
-	Cracked or unresponsive screen
-	Buttons, ports, or speakers not working
-	Won’t power on, charge, or connect to iTunes
-	Audio or touchscreen malfunction
-	Missing parts or severe damage
-	Water damage
