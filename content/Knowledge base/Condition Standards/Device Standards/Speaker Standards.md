## Brand New:
-	Factory sealed in original box
-	Never used or powered on
-	Includes all original accessories (power cable, charger, manuals)
-	No cosmetic or functional issues
## Like New:
-	Looks flawless — no scratches, dents, or scuffs
-	Sound quality clear and balanced — no distortion or static
-	All features work perfectly (Bluetooth, Wi-Fi, voice control, buttons, etc.)
-	Fully functional
## Good:
-	Light cosmetic wear (small scuffs or faint marks)
-	Sound and controls work properly
-	All connection methods (Bluetooth, aux, Wi-Fi, etc.) function correctly
-	May show light use but no major flaws
-	Fully functional
## Fair:
-	Noticeable wear (scratches, dents, or faded finish)
-	Plays sound normally but may have minor cosmetic or control issues
-	All essential functions (volume, power, playback, charging) work
-	Fully functional
## Damaged:
-	Distorted or no sound
-	Broken buttons, ports, or casing
-	Bluetooth or Wi-Fi not connecting
-	Battery won’t charge or speaker won’t power on
-	Missing parts (charging cable, cover, etc.)
-	Water damage or severe physical damage
