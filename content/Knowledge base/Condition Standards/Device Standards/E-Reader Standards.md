## Brand New:
-	Factory sealed in original box
-	Never activated or used
-	Includes all original accessories (charger, cable, manuals)
-	No cosmetic or functional issues
## Like New:
-	Looks flawless — no scratches, dents, or scuffs
-	Screen clear and even — no cracks, lines, or discoloration
-	Touchscreen, buttons, and lighting all work perfectly
-	Fully functional
## Good:
-	Light cosmetic wear (small scuffs or faint marks)
-	Screen in great shape — no cracks or major blemishes
-	All core functions (touch, Wi-Fi, page turn, lighting) work properly
-	Fully functional
## Fair:
-	Noticeable wear (scratches, faded finish, or small dents)
-	Screen lights up and works, but may have minor cosmetic flaws
-	All essential features (touch, page turn, power, etc.) still work
-	Fully functional

## Damaged:
-	Cracked, unresponsive, or flickering screen
-	Buttons, touchscreen, or lighting not working
-	Won’t power on, charge, or connect to Wi-Fi
-	Missing parts (charger, cover, etc.)
-	Water damage or severe physical damage
