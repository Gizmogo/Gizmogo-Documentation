## Brand New:
-	Factory sealed in original box
-	Never activated or used
-	Includes all original accessories (controllers, cables, manuals)
-	No damage, no missing parts
## Like New:
-	Looks flawless — no scratches, scuffs, or signs of use
-	Fully functional
-	No overheating, freezing, or connection issues
-	All ports, buttons, and controllers work perfectly
-	Includes original cables and accessories
-	Fully functional
## Good:
-	Light wear (minor scratches or scuffs)
-	Fully functional with no performance issues
-	Controllers, buttons, and ports all working properly
-	No internal or display issues (for handhelds)
-	May be missing original packaging
-	Fully functional
## Fair:
-	Noticeable wear (scratches, worn buttons, minor dents, or discoloration)
-	Console powers on and runs but may have minor issues (fan noise, loose ports, cosmetic damage)
-	Controllers may show wear but still functional
-	May be missing some accessories
-	Fully functional
## Damaged:
-	Cracked or broken housing
-	Won’t power on, boot, or read games/discs
-	Controller, port, or button malfunction
-	Overheating, freezing, or display problems (for handhelds)
-	Missing key components (controllers, hard drive, power supply)
-	Water damage or severe physical damage
