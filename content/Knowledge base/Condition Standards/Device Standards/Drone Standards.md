## Brand New:
-	Factory sealed in original box
-	Never activated or flown
-	Includes all original accessories (remote, charger, propellers, manuals)
-	No cosmetic or functional issues
## Like New:
-	Looks flawless — no scratches, scuffs, or signs of use
-	Camera, gimbal, and sensors fully functional
-	Flies smoothly — no calibration or stability issues
-	Battery holds excellent charge
-	Fully functional
## Good:
-	Light cosmetic wear (minor scuffs or marks on body or propellers)
-	Camera and gimbal work properly — no cracks or tilt issues
-	Flies normally with stable GPS and responsive controls
-	Battery in good condition and holds charge
-	Fully functional
## Fair:
-	Noticeable wear (scratches, small cracks, or faded paint)
-	Flies and records normally but may have minor stability or signal issues
-	Propellers, camera, or gimbal show light wear but still usable
-	May be missing minor accessories (prop guards, extra props, etc.)
-	Fully functional
## Damaged:
-	Cracked or broken body or propeller arms
-	Gimbal, camera, or sensors not working
-	Battery won’t charge or drone won’t power on
-	Flight instability, lost GPS, or connection failure
-	Water damage or missing major parts (remote, battery, camera)
