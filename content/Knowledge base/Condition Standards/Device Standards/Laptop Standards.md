## Brand New:
-	Factory sealed in original box
-	Never activated or used
-	Includes original charger and accessories
-	No damage, no missing keys, and no performance issues
## Like New:
-	Looks flawless — no visible wear
-	No scratches, dents, or scuffs
-	All keys, ports, trackpad, and webcam work perfectly
-	Display free from dead pixels, discoloration, or pressure marks
-	Fully functional
-	**Battery health 90%+**
## Good:
-	Light cosmetic wear (minor scuffs or faint scratches)
-	Screen in great shape — no cracks, lines, or major blemishes
-	Keyboard, trackpad, and ports all functional
-	No missing parts
-	Fully operational
-	**Battery health 85%+**
## Fair:
-	Noticeable wear (scratches, worn keys, dents, or faded paint)
-	Screen lights up with no major display issues
-	Some cosmetic flaws but all major functions work
-	May have replaced parts or light frame wear
-	**Battery health below 85%**
## Damaged: 
-	Cracked or damaged screen
-	Keyboard, trackpad, ports, or hinges not working properly
-	Display issues (dead pixels, flickering, water damage)
-	Missing parts or screws
-	Won’t power on, charge, or boot properly
-	Severe frame damage or overheating issues
-	**Battery health below 70*
