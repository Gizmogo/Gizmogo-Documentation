## Brand New:
-	Factory sealed in original box
-	Never activated or used
-	Includes all original accessories (lens cap, strap, charger, cables, manuals)
-	No cosmetic or functional issues
## Like New:
-	Looks flawless — no scratches, dents, or wear
-	Lens, viewfinder, and LCD screen are crystal clear (no dust, scratches, or dead pixels)
-	Autofocus, shutter, flash, and all buttons work perfectly
-	Fully functional
## Good:
-	Light cosmetic wear (small scuffs or faint marks, not visible from 8" away)
-	Lens and screen in great shape — no cracks or deep scratches
-	All functions (autofocus, shutter, zoom, flash) work properly
-	Fully functional
## Fair:
-	Noticeable wear (scratches, worn buttons, faded paint, or minor dents)
-	Screen and viewfinder light up with no major defects
-	Some cosmetic flaws but still takes photos normally
-	Lens may show small dust particles not affecting performance
-	Fully functional
## Damaged:
-	Cracked or non-functional lens, screen, or viewfinder
-	Shutter, zoom, or autofocus not working
-	Flash or power button malfunction
-	Missing parts (battery, lens cap, memory card door, etc.)
-	Water damage, sensor issues, or camera won’t power on
