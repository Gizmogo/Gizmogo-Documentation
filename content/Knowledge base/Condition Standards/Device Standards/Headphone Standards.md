## Brand New:
-	Factory sealed in original box
-	Never used or worn
-	Includes all original accessories (charging case, cable, ear tips, manuals)
-	No cosmetic or functional issues
## Like New:
-	Looks flawless — no scratches, scuffs, or wear
-	Sound quality crystal clear — no distortion or imbalance
-	All features work perfectly (Bluetooth, noise canceling, touch controls, etc.)
-	Fully functional
## Good:
-	Light cosmetic wear (small scuffs or faint marks)
-	Sound and microphone work properly
-	All buttons, sensors, and connectivity features functional
-	May show light signs of use but no major flaws
-	Fully functional
## Fair:
-	Noticeable wear (scratches, faded finish, or worn ear cushions/tips)
-	Plays sound normally but may have minor cosmetic flaws
-	All essential features work (Bluetooth, charging, audio output, etc.)
-	Fully functional
## Damaged:
-	One or both sides not working
-	Broken headband, charging case, or connector
-	Poor or no Bluetooth connection
-	Distorted sound, microphone failure, or charging issues
-	Missing parts (ear tips, case, cable, etc.)
-	Water damage
