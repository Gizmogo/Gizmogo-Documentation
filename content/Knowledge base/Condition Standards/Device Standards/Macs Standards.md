## Brand New:
- Factory sealed in original Apple box
- Never activated or used
- Includes original keyboard, mouse, and power cable
- No damage or missing accessories
## Like New:
- Looks flawless — no scratches, dents, or scuffs
- Screen crystal clear (no dead pixels, lines, or discoloration)
- Ports, speakers, and webcam work perfectly
- Fully functional
## Good:
- Light cosmetic wear (small scuffs or faint marks)
- Display in excellent condition — no cracks or major blemishes
- All ports, buttons, and internal components work properly
- May have minor wear on stand or frame
- Fully functional
## Fair:
- Noticeable cosmetic wear (scratches, dents, or frame discoloration)
- Screen lights up and works, but may have minor flaws (small spots, faint image retention)
- Keyboard or mouse may show wear but still functional
- All essential components operational
- May include light fan noise or minor cosmetic damage
- Fully functional
## Damaged:
- Cracked or non-functional display
- Issues with power, logic board, graphics, or overheating
- Ports, speakers, or webcam not working
- Missing parts (stand, screws, accessories)
- Won’t boot, display, or function properly
- Water damage, severe dents, or broken glass
