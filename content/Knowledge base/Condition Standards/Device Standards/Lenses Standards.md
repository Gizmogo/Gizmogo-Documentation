## Brand New:
-	Factory sealed in original box
-	Never mounted or used
-	Includes all original accessories (caps, hood, pouch, manuals)
-	No cosmetic or optical defects
## Like New:
-	Looks flawless — no scratches, scuffs, or marks
-	Glass elements crystal clear — no haze, moisture, or dust
-	Autofocus, aperture, and stabilization work perfectly
-	Mount shows no signs of use
-	Fully functional
## Good:
-	Light cosmetic wear (small scuffs or faint marks on barrel)
-	Glass clean — no scratches or chips affecting image quality
-	Autofocus, aperture, and zoom mechanisms work properly
-	Fully functional
## Fair:
-	Noticeable wear (scratches, fading, or small dents on barrel)
-	Minor internal dust or small marks that do not impact photo quality
-	Mount and controls intact but may show use
-	Fully functional
## Damaged:
-	Cracked, chipped, or heavily scratched glass
-	Autofocus, zoom, or aperture not working properly
-	Loose or bent mount
-	Severe internal dust, haze, or fungus
-	Water damage or lens won’t focus/take sharp images
