# Phones & Tablets

Brand New:
- Factory sealed in original box
- Never activated or used
- No carrier lock, blacklist, or payments owed
 
Like New:
- Looks flawless — no wear at all
- No cracks, scratches, dents, or scuffs
- Fully functional
- Battery health 95%+
 
Good:
 - Light wear (small scratches or scuffs, not obvious from 8" away)
 - No cracks or chips in glass
 - Screen works perfectly (no burn, white spots, dead pixels)
 - Fully functional
 - Battery health 85%+
 
Fair:
 - Noticeable wear (scratches, dents, or mild frame damage)
 - No cracked glass
 - Screen lights up with no major defects
 - Fully functional
 - Battery health 80%+
 
Damaged: 
 - Cracked screen or back glass
 - Display issues (dead pixels, white spots, water damage)
 - Buttons, ports, speakers, or Face/Touch ID not working
 - Missing parts or bent frame
 - Won’t connect to SIM/Wi -Fi/Bluetooth OR won’t power on
 - Battery health below 80%
